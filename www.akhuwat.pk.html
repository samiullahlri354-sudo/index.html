About Akhuwat University

Our Vision

To create a society based on the principles of compassion, equality, and justice by providing free quality education to all, regardless of their financial background.

Our Mission

To eliminate financial barriers to education by offering completely free degree programs, fostering academic excellence, and developing socially responsible leaders.

Free Education

At Akhuwat University, we believe that education is a fundamental right. All our programs are offered completely free of cost, including tuition, books, and other academic expenses.

Our Leadership

Dr. Amjad Saqib Founder & CEO Akhuwat Foundation

Dr. Amjad Saqib

Founder & CEO, Akhuwat Foundation

Dr. Amjad Saqib is the visionary founder and CEO of Akhuwat Foundation, one of the largest interest-free microfinance organizations in the world. His dedication to eradicating poverty through compassion and empowerment has transformed millions of lives across Pakistan.

Under his leadership, Akhuwat has expanded its mission to include education, healthcare, and social services, with Akhuwat University being a flagship initiative to provide free quality education to deserving students.

Dr. Saqib's innovative approach to social development has earned him international recognition and numerous prestigious awards.
